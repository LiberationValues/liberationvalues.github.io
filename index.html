DiscriminatoryValues

What is DiscriminatoryValues?

DiscriminatoryValues is a quiz that has the aim of measuring you on twelve different discrimination-based values. You will be presented by a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will be compared to the maximum possible for each value, thus giving you a percentage. Answer honestly!

There are questions in the test.

What are the twelve values?

There are sis independent axes - Racism, Sexism, Ageism, Homophobia, Transphobia, and Fatphobia - and each has two opposing values assigned to them. They are:

White Supremacy (Racism)
White supremacists believev that the white race is superior to every other race.

Black Supremacy (Racism)
Black supremacists believe that the black race is superior to every other race.

Matriarchy (Sexism)
Matriarchists believe that social customs and/or laws should benefit the woman more than the man.

Patriarchy (Sexism)
Patriarchists believe that social customs and/or laws should benefit the man more than the woman.

Jeunism (Ageism)
Jeunists believe that the law of the land should give youbger people more rights than older people.

Adultism (Ageism)
Adultists believe that the law of the land should give older people more rights than younger people.

Heteronormatism (Homophobia)
Heteronormative people believe that social customs, traditions, and even laws should favor heterosexual lifestyles/individuals over homosexual lifestyles/individuals.

Homonormatism (Homophobia)
Homonormative people believe that social customs, traditions, and even laws should favor homosexual lifestyles/individuals over heterosexual lifestyles/individuals.

Cisnormatism (Transphobia)
Cisnormative people believe that gender identity should be fully based on chromosomes and birth gender. Usually, people in this category believe trans people are invalid.

Transnormatism (Transphobia)
Transnormative people believe that cisgendered people/cisgenderism should be looked down upon or even discriminated against. People in this category believe trans people are superior.

Fatphobic (Fatphobia)
Fatphobic people believe people who are not able-bodied and/or obese are inferior to thin and/or able-bodied people. People here believe there is nothing wrong with fatshaming.

Thinphobic (Fatphobia)
Thinphobic people believe fat people are naturally superior to thin people and that thinshaming should replace fatshaming.
